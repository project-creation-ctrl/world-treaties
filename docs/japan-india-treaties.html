India's Gain

How Japan's Treaties Benefit India

From bullet trains to semiconductor supply chains — Japan's bilateral engagement has produced concrete, measurable outcomes across six strategic domains that shape India's development trajectory.

🏗️
Infrastructure (ODA)
Japan's JICA loans funded Delhi Metro, Mumbai Metro, Ahmedabad Metro, Chennai peripheral road, and the Mumbai–Ahmedabad bullet train corridor. ODA loans are at 0.1% interest over 50 years — concessional terms no other partner offers.
¥3.5 Trillion
💹
Trade (CEPA 2011)
India's most comprehensive FTA eliminated tariffs on 94% of goods. Bilateral trade grew from $8B in 2011 to over $20B by 2023. India gained market access for pharma, chemicals, engineering goods, and IT services.
$20B+ Trade
🔬
Technology Transfer
Science & Technology Cooperation Agreement, ISRO–JAXA collaboration, and the 2023 Semiconductor Partnership. India gains access to Japanese precision manufacturing and chip design expertise amid global tech competition.
9 Tech Pacts
🛡️
Defence & Security
ACSA (2020) enables mutual logistical support between forces. 2+2 Ministerial Dialogue, joint naval exercises, and Quad membership create a strategic hedge against Chinese assertiveness in the Indo-Pacific.
6 Defence Pacts
🌿
Clean Energy
Japan–India Clean Energy Partnership (2022) channels $42B into solar, green hydrogen, ammonia fuel, and battery storage — directly supporting India's COP26 commitment of 500GW renewable capacity by 2030.
$42B Pledged
🎓
Education & Culture
MEXT scholarships for Indian students, Japanese language centres, IIT–Tokyo University collaboration, and cultural exchange programs. People-to-people ties create a long-term soft power foundation for the relationship.
5 Cultural Pacts
