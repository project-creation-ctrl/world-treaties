Impact on India

How Maldives Treaties Shape India's Strategy

Every Maldives diplomatic move — from signing a Chinese harbour deal to inviting Indian military — directly affects India's Indian Ocean security architecture, trade route protection, and regional power projection.

🛡️
Strategic Maritime Location
Maldives sits astride India's primary sea lanes — 8 degrees and One-and-a-Half degree channels handle 80% of India's oil imports. Any hostile naval presence in Maldivian waters threatens India's energy security directly.
80% India Oil Route
🔭
HIMNAFUSHI Defence Pact
India operates a coast guard base, a dornier maritime patrol aircraft, and two helicopters in Maldives under bilateral defence agreements. These provide India real-time maritime domain awareness across the central Indian Ocean.
3 Military Assets
🏗️
India's Infrastructure Presence
India financed the Greater Male Connectivity Project (bridge + road), Hanimaadhoo International Airport, Addu development projects, and the Maldives' National University hospital — maintaining deep economic entrenchment.
$800M LOC
🌊
Climate Leadership via MV
Maldives is the world's most vocal climate-change advocate — its UNFCCC leadership strengthens the broader South Asian climate bloc that India co-leads. MV's SIDS voice amplifies India's climate diplomacy at COP summits.
8 Climate Pacts
🇨🇳
China Risk — "India Out" Campaign
President Muizzu's 2023 "India Out" campaign and request to withdraw Indian military personnel created the sharpest India–Maldives rupture since 1988. China immediately stepped in with infrastructure offers — textbook string-of-pearls strategy.
$1B+ China Pledge
🚑
Health Diplomacy
India provides free medicines, medical specialist visits, air ambulance services, and AIIMS-trained doctors to Maldives under health cooperation agreements. COVID vaccine diplomacy — India sent 100,000 Covishield doses in 2021 — cemented this dependency.
100K Vaccines 2021
